Beef Stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can of Bush's Baked Beans
  • 250 ml of Milk
  • Corn
  • Gas

Steps:

  1. Put all the ingredients together in a group
  2. Put all the ingredients in a strong blender for about 5 minutes
  3. Put in the microwave for about 2 seconds
  4. Drink immediately after microwave
  5. The beef stew will come soon
